About Me

My name is Vin and I'm based in Auckland, NZ. I'm a keen photographer and love to shoot all types of subjects ranging from portaits, architecture, wildlife and sports. I used to be a big gamer but now I mostly just play TFT and any interesting multiplayer games my friends decide to play. Recently I have picked up rock climbing as a new hobby after getting bored of going to the gym and its been pretty fun so far.

I based this design off of one of my favourite games of all time(Pokemon Emerald). Feel free to look around and read my blog posts once they go up and watch my blog evolve throughout the duration of foundations! A few of my links can be found below to see my work.
