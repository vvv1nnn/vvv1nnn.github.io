Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

My way of keeping my physical well being in good shape is by going to the gym 2-3 times a week and going bouldering 2 times a week. It keeps me in good shape and healthy and in a good mindset to tackle anything thrown at me.

My spiritual wellbeing is stregthened by going to the temple every weekend and joining in the activities at the temple.
